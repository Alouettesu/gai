This is my personal blog, where I will share my thoughts and experiences
on programming and other topics that interest me.

What my nickname "Gai" means? It is a Chinese word, which means "calcium". I chose this nickname because I like calcium, and I think it is a very important element for human health. Calcium is essential for strong bones and teeth, and it also plays a crucial role in muscle function, nerve transmission, and blood clotting. I hope that my blog can be as useful and essential as calcium for my readers.
